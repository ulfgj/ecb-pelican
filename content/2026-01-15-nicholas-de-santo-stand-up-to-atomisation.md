Nicholas De Santo is an award winning Italian-born comic based in London, England. 

As one of a handful of conservative stand-up comedians in the UK, he challenges the leftist, mainstream agenda with counter-culture comedy and political satire.

Nicholas De Santo has over 100K subscribers on YouTube and his routines have been viewed more than 10 million times. In 2019, he was UK's New Act of the Year (NATYS) finalist at Hackney Empire Theatre, London.
