14 February 2023

Tom Deacon & Fiona Ridgewell

 

We are back at The Black Sheep our first show of 2023, and are delighted to have Tom Deacon paying us another visit, with special guest Fiona Ridgewell.

 

Tom Deacon 

Tom has been performing stand-up comedy for 15 years, from all of the clubs in the UK to sell out shows in New Zealand. An accomplished presenter on BBC Radio 1 to hosting the F1 Esports Pro Championship on Sky F1. You're in safe hands with whatever he's preforming on. 

'A mighty fine comic' The Guardian 

'With looks, confidence, charm and great material; expect other comics to lynch him' The Sunday Times

 

Fiona Ridgewell

Fiona Ridgewell is an upbeat conversational comedian.

In February 2020 her show “Contender” was nominated for best debut show at Leicester Comedy Festival, in the same year she she was a Funny Women Finalist.

“Fiona has a direct cheeky sense of humour” Steve Bennett Chortle“Fiona Ridgewell has funny bones and an easy warmth on stage.” Julia Chamberlain
